About Us

We are a group of students from STEM Qena working on a smart and affordable water filter, designed to improve the quality of drinking water.

Our goal is to solve the problem of water pollution in areas where clean water isn’t always available, while promoting environmental sustainability and community health.

The filter removes harmful substances and makes water safe to drink, using simple materials and smart design that can be easily replicated in different regions.

We aim to help people access clean water easily and safely, ensuring that communities in need benefit from this essential resource without major costs.

The Problem

Access to clean and safe drinking water remains a major challenge in many parts of the world, especially in rural and underserved communities.

Water sources are often contaminated with pollutants such as heavy metals, bacteria, high salinity, and acidity levels, making them dangerous for human consumption.

Existing filtration systems are often expensive, complex, or require advanced maintenance, which limits their accessibility for low-income families.

These challenges highlight the urgent need for a low-cost, efficient, and easy-to-use filtration system that can remove harmful substances and improve water quality without relying on complex infrastructure.
